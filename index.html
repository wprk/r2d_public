The project

Our aim is simple, we want to make learning to drive easier. We believe by helping learners get the right instructor for them and offering you the best tools available to learn we can help you pass your test quicker (and cheaper) and help you become the best driver you can be.

Instructor Search

Find the right instructor for you in our list of approved instructors, you can search by Customer Reviews, Gender, Experience, Qualifications and more.

Easy Booking

Book all your lessons online using our clever calendar tool. With email and text reminders we can make sure you never miss a lesson again.

Progress Feedback

Feel like you're having lessons but not making progress? Your instructor will highlight your strengths and weaknesses and show you your progress across every aspect of learning to drive.

Learning Zone

Our fun learning zone with practice theory questions and hazard perception games to let you learn everything you need to know without the stress.

About us

When I was learning to drive I got so frustrated at the lack of feedback I was getting. I was paying £22 every hour and had to just trust my instructor wasn't just taking me for a mug. I would go out driving with my parents and I wouldn't know what to practice. We're hoping that road2driving will change that by giving you the information you need to learn to drive better and quicker.
